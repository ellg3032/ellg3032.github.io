My color choices represent my personal identity because purple and green are both colors I've liked for a very long time, and that I think look really good. My typography choices represent my personal identity because when I saw this font, I immediately resonated with it, telling me it was the right font for the website. Accessibility requirements influenced my choice of text colors for the colors I chose, because I had to have a certain contrast ratio in order to make it readable for people with visual impairments. The main challenges I found were finding things that represented myself, however making them more accessible was not really an issue. What surprised me is that creating a color scheme was really difficult, and I'm still not convinced this is the right color scheme. I will apply this system to creative nice looking elements on my website with good contrasts. I might expand this system to create even better looking elements. Most of it feels pretty flexible, other than the text sizes.
